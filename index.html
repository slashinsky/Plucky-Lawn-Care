Our story

Small company.
Big care.

Plucky Lawn Care was founded right here in Decatur by Marcus and Ellie Webb — a husband-and-wife team who got tired of watching neighbors deal with unreliable, impersonal lawn services. We started with a single truck, a few good tools, and a lot of patience.

Today we serve over 150 residential and small commercial clients across Decatur, Avondale Estates, and Stone Mountain. Every job still gets our personal attention — because to us, your yard isn't a ticket number, it's someone's home.
